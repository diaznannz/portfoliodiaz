Habitus

Habitus is a UI/UX design project that adopts the *Greenceleration* approach— a concept of sustainable and eco-friendly digital acceleration. This project is designed to support digital marketing strategies that not only focus on results but also consider ecological impact and users’ digital consumption habits.

Project Goals

  • Introduce a digital marketing concept aligned with sustainability principles.
  • Strengthen a green, clean, and futuristic visual identity.
  • Enhance user experience in understanding the value of an “eco-driven business.”
