Bird UP

BirdUp is a digital platform concept that provides comprehensive information about various bird species. The design focuses on an appealing visual layout and intuitive navigation to help users easily explore the diversity of birds around the world. This project highlights illustrative elements and natural colors that reflect the essence of nature and the freedom of birds in their habitats.

Project Goals

  • Increase awareness of biodiversity, especially bird species.
  • Create an interactive learning experience through engaging visual displays.
  • Present a modern and minimalist UI/UX design.

Tools Used

Figma (UI/UX Design) and vector illustrations for bird icons.
