EduSign

EduSign is an inclusive learning app concept designed to support students with special needs, especially those who use sign language. The app aims to make education more accessible, engaging, and equitable for all learners through interactive and visual-based learning experiences.

Main Objectives

  • Enhance educational accessibility through digital sign language integration.
  • Promote equality and inclusion in learning environments.
  • Provide an engaging, visually-driven, and interactive learning platform.

Design Concept

The design focuses on simplicity and clarity, using soft colors and intuitive navigation to ensure all users can learn comfortably. The visual hierarchy emphasizes sign language videos and learning modules, with accessible typography and high-contrast layouts to support readability.
